MetArtworld:

~Example of humans interacting with technology

The Evolution of Trust:

~An interactive experience through a short game which explains the concept of the "prisoner's dilemma" in depth.
~The website layout is very clean, and very easy and instinctual to navigate, as it mainly proceeds in a forward momentum.
~There is a lot of creative and fun animation, such as the background, as well as the avatars.




Picture of a Hotdog:

~A satirical website with seemingly no context. Surprisingly well designed in its simplicity,
yet balanced by the occasional deviations from proffessionalism, using default fonts to label.
~Easy navigation and very humorous approach.
