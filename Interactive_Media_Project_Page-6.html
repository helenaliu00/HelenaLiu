Designing a game that can be created with the use of P5 and dice-rolling. The game rules are stated below (created with partner -Dreamy):

  • More number higher = shape more sides (they correspond)
  • Odd = even # sides
    Even = vice versa
  • Colours: 1-2 = white, 3-4 = pink, 5-6 = black
  • 2x dice: both have 6 sides
  • One determines the length, the other determines the width
  • Placement of the shapes are random
