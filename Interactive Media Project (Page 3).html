Week Two


Crazy Eights:

Activity done in class where we fold creases into an A3 page to create 8 grids.
A timer is then set, and the wireframe sketching begins. One minute per grid!

At the end of this activity, class work was moved and stuck up onto a wall,
where we were then expected to elaborate and expand on our ideas.


Paper Prototyping:

Oh! Surprise, my paper prototyping looks so different to the ideas I'd created in Crazy Eights.
For the final activity of this week's class, we were assigned the task of mapping out
our wireframes and really digging into a design that we would like to fabricate.
We were given templates to work off from, in the formats of both webpage & mobile.

I was inspired heavily by the creativity of all my peers.
Instead of focusing on a simply "aesthetically pleasing" (but perhaps boring) website,
I decided that I would try something a little more humble and humorous!
Especially as I am less fluent with all the intricacies of coding language,
I wanted to focus on creating something that would keep my engagement, and allow me to have a little fun.
